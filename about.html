Restaurant

AM/PM opened was with the idea that comfort food can be eaten anywhere and at at anytime. First opened in the heart of Downtown Toronto, AM/PM is a place for people to take a load off from work and relieve stress. We offer comfort food for the daytime as well as for the evening and night. Which is why we the restaurant is called AM/PM.

Chef

Our head chef, Andre, was born and raised in Toronto, Canada. He has worked in different cities around North America, including New York, Vancouver, and Los Angeles. Andre understands the benefits of a good meal has on our stress levels. He knows that stepping away from your work for a bite is always a good way to help eliminate frustration.

Owners

The owners are a group of Web Developers that wanted somewhere to go that would help them work through coding problems. They all realized that taking a break to get something to eat is always the best way to restart the debugging engines. They partnered with Chef Andre, who, at the time, was looking to open a restaurant in Toronto. After 4 years of success, they decided to open a second location in The Beaches neighbourhood of Toronto.
